# Website Revamp Brief

## Objective

Prepare a future redesign brief for `vaultwares-v1` so the public site can later reflect the Identity Manager product accurately and credibly.

## This Phase

Research and brief only. No site implementation.

## Goals

- modernize the VaultWares visual and narrative presence
- explain the privacy-first fictional-identity proposition clearly
- support conversion, education, and credibility
- create room for future product-family expansion

## Required Outputs

- future site information architecture
- homepage narrative outline
- feature page list
- conversion funnel proposals
- required asset inventory from product/design teams

## Proposed Site Structure

- home
- product overview
- browser extension
- desktop and mobile apps
- security and privacy model
- pricing placeholder / future plans
- education/blog
- FAQ

## Key Messaging Constraints

- do not imply impersonation or fraud use cases
- do not advertise unavailable billing/telecom/network features as active
- explain fictional personas in privacy-protective terms
- make encryption, trust, and recovery understandable

## Deferred Implementation Gate

Do not redesign the public site until:

- brand direction is approved
- product screenshots and shell designs are stable
- core messaging hierarchy is approved
- SEO plan is aligned with the site structure
