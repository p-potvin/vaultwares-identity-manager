# SEO and Content Plan Brief

## Objective

Define an SEO and educational content strategy that supports product discovery and trust-building without overpromising unavailable features.

## Key Questions

- which search intents matter most for launch?
- what content clusters support the privacy-first identity proposition?
- which education topics can convert without sounding alarmist?
- how should fictional identity generation be explained publicly?

## Required Outputs

- keyword cluster map
- landing page topic list
- editorial calendar concept
- internal linking strategy
- measurement plan for content performance

## Content Themes

- privacy basics and data minimization
- sign-up hygiene and identity compartmentalization
- secure credential management
- understanding fictional identities vs impersonation
- recovery, device trust, and encrypted sync education

## Deferred Implementation Gate

Full SEO execution should align with:

- website revamp brief completion
- approved brand copy
- stable visual assets and product screenshots
