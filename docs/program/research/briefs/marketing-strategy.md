# Marketing Strategy Brief

## Objective

Define how VaultWares Identity Manager should be positioned, explained, and launched once the product foundation is credible.

## Key Questions

- who are the primary user segments?
- how should the product differentiate from password managers and alias-email tools?
- what language best explains fictional identities without sounding deceptive or abusive?
- what educational angles support trust and conversion?

## Required Outputs

- audience segmentation memo
- messaging hierarchy
- launch narrative options
- channel strategy for publicity and partnerships
- credibility/trust asset list needed from product and design

## Candidate Audience Segments

- privacy-conscious mainstream users
- power users tired of data brokerage and spam
- users already managing multiple alias emails or compartmentalized accounts

## Deferred Implementation Gate

Marketing execution can start only after:

- brand direction is approved
- extension/UI visuals are credible
- product boundary language is final
- at least one stable product demo exists
